About Us

TATTOO STUDIO ~NEON~

When you think about placing a tattoo, it is useful to consider several factors. Before you start thinking about the location and color of the tattoo, it is important to clearly know what kind of tattoo you would like to have. When it concerns your first or any subsequent tattoos, we understand better than anyone that this can sometimes be a difficult choice. Are you opting for something personal and creating your own design? Or would you prefer to take a look at people who came before you? At Tattoo Neon we think it is very important that we offer you a wide range of tattoos examples en tattoo stylez that we can offer. We add new tattoo examples to this almost daily, so that the examples always remain up-to-date. Our Tattoo studio have very creatyve artists with high range of expiriance.

THE SHOP

Nowadays, more and more people are choosing to get a tattoo, as a result, there are plenty of examples available. To make it easy for you, at Tattoo Bob we divide all tattoo examples into different tattoo styles. To give you a good idea about these different styles and examples, we have added extra information about the relevant tattoo style in our gallery. Think of the history, origin and meaning of these styles. In general, tattoos for each individual have their own meaning that they carry with them for the rest of their lives. Therefore, the meaning of the different tattoo examples may be different for you than for the one who has already put the tattoo. At Tattoo Neon we like to listen to your personal story, in order to make a personal design for you from the tattoo example. Have you already made a design yourself? Then we ensure that we tattoo it on your body exactly according to your wishes. View the work of our tattoo artists below! Is your desired tattoo example or style not listed? Or do you need more information? Then call or email us! Of course we also design what you want.
